Start Menu

The start menu of the brick breaker game offers players options to begin playing, view instructions, or exit the game. Upon launching the game, players are greeted with a simple interface displaying these menu choices. Selecting the play option starts the game, placing the player's paddle at the bottom of the screen and releasing the ball to initiate gameplay. The start menu provides a clear and intuitive entry point for users to engage with the game.

Points Table

In the brick breaker game, points are awarded based on the type of brick broken. Each brick hit by the ball earns the player a specific number of points. For example, basic bricks might award 10 points, while more resilient bricks could yield higher scores. Additionally, players can earn bonus points for completing levels quickly or achieving special feats, such as breaking all bricks with a single ball. The points table tracks and displays the player's current score throughout the game, encouraging competition and mastery of gameplay mechanics.

What I Have Done

In developing this brick breaker game for my portfolio, I designed and implemented the game mechanics, including paddle movement, ball physics, and brick interactions. I programmed the start menu interface to provide a user-friendly experience for navigating the game's options. Additionally, I created the scoring system, ensuring that players are rewarded appropriately for their achievements within the game. The project involved a blend of game design, programming, and user interface development to deliver an engaging and polished brick breaker experience.

Gameplay

During gameplay, players control the paddle using keyboard controls or touchscreen gestures, depending on the platform. The ball bounces off the walls and the paddle, interacting with the bricks. Different types of bricks have varying durability levels, requiring multiple hits to destroy. Power-ups may occasionally drop from destroyed bricks, offering temporary advantages such as increasing paddle size or granting multi-ball capabilities.

End of Game

The game continues until either all the bricks are destroyed, or the player loses all their lives by letting the ball fall off the bottom of the screen. Upon completion of the game, players can view their final score and compare it with previous attempts.
